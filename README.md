# Snooker Tubey Tracker

Welcome to the Snooker Tubey Tracker! This web application is designed to help you keep track of used stations during your games of Snooker Tubey. It's built to be fast, easy to use, and works on any device with a modern web browser, including your phone and tablet.

This tracker can be installed on your device's home screen and used offline, so you don't need an internet connection to play.

## Features

- **Responsive Design:** The layout automatically adjusts to fit your device's screen, whether it's a desktop, tablet, or mobile phone.
- **Offline Functionality:** Once loaded, the tracker works entirely offline, so you can use it anywhere.
- **Add to Home Screen:** You can add the tracker to your device's home screen for quick and easy access, just like a native app.
- **Game Tier Selection:** Choose between Advanced, Intermediate, and Casual tiers to match your house rules.
- **Station Validation:** The tracker helps you verify if a station is valid for the potted ball's colour.
- **Searchable Station List:** Quickly search for any station to check its lines, colours, and usage status.
- **Overground Wildcard:** A dedicated button to help you manage the Overground Wildcard rule.
- **Reset Game:** Easily start a new game at any time.

## How to Install on Your Mobile Device

To get the best experience, you can add this tracker to your home screen. This will make it launch like a regular app and ensure it's always available offline.

### For iOS/iPadOS (Safari)

1.  **Open Safari:** Launch the Safari browser and navigate to the URL where the Snooker Tubey Tracker is hosted.
2.  **Tap the Share Button:** Tap the "Share" icon at the bottom of the screen. It looks like a square with an arrow pointing out of the top.
3.  **Select "Add to Home Screen":** Scroll down the list of options and tap on **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4.  **Confirm the Name:** You can customize the name that will appear on your home screen, or leave it as "Tubey Tracker".
5.  **Tap "Add":** Tap the "Add" button in the top-right corner.

The tracker will now appear on your home screen.

### For Android (Chrome)

1.  **Open Chrome:** Launch the Chrome browser and navigate to the URL where the Snooker Tubey Tracker is hosted.
2.  **Tap the Menu Button:** Tap the three-dot menu icon in the top-right corner of the browser.
3.  **Select "Install App" or "Add to Home screen":** Look for an option that says **"Install App"** (or it may say **"Add to Home screen"**). Tap on it.
4.  **Confirm the Installation:** A pop-up will appear asking you to confirm. Tap **"Install"**.

The tracker will be added to your app drawer and home screen, ready to use anytime.

Enjoy your game!
